## Definition ([[Kal2021]], pg. 10): Borel $\sigma$-field

We endow a [[metric space]] or [[topological space]] $S$ with its [[Borel σ-field]] $\mathcal{B}_S$ generated by the [[topology]] in $S$, unless a [[σ-field]] is otherwise specified.
