## Definition 3.1 ([[Bas2024]], pg. 17): definition
Let $X$ be a set and $\mathcal{A}$ a [[Ïƒ-algebra]] consisting of subsets of $X$. A [[measure]] on the [[measurable space]] $(X, \mathcal{A})$ is a function $\mu: \mathcal{A} \to [0, \infty]$ such that
1. $\mu(\emptyset)=0$;
2. if $A_i \in \mathcal{A}, i=1, 2 , \ldots$ are [[pairwise disjoint]], then 
$$
\mu(\cup_{i=1}^\infty A_i) = \sum_{i=1}^\infty \mu(A_i).
$$
