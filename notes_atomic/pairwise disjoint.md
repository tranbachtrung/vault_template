## Definition ([[Bas2024]], pg. 17): pairwise disjoint
Let $\{A_i\}$ be a collection of sets. We say the $A_i$'s are pairwise disjoint if that $A_i \cap A_j = \emptyset$ if $i \neq j$. We say that the collection is $\{A_i\}$ disjoint.
