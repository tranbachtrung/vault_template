## Definition 2.1 ([[Bas2024]], pg. 11): algebra
An algebra is a collection $\mathcal{A}$ of subsets of $X$ such that
1. $\emptyset \in \mathcal{A}$ and $X \in \mathcal{A}$
2. if $A \in \mathcal{A}$ then $A^c = X - A \in \mathcal{A}$
3. if $A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n \in \mathcal{A}$, then $\cup_{i=1}^n A_i \in \mathcal{A}$, and $\cap_{i=1}^n A_i \in \mathcal{A}$.
