## Definition ([[Kal2021]], pg. 10): $\pi$-system
A class $\mathcal{D}$ of subsets of a space $S$ is a [[λ-system]] if it contains $S$ and is closed under [[proper difference]] and [[increasing and decreasing sets|increasing limit]], that is:
1. $S \in \mathcal{D}$; 
2. $A, B \in \mathcal{D}$ with $A \supset B$ implies $A \backslash B \in D$;
3. $A_1, A_2, \ldots \in \mathcal{D}$ and $A_n \uparrow A$ implies $A \in D$.
